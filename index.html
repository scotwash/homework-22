Venus Fly Trap

Anna Beth

February 12, 2025

Venus flytraps are typically green but depending on genetics can be different colors like red or purple.

Venus flytraps, despite their behavior and appearence are still plants and are born via seeds. They also take five years to reach their adult state.

Venus flytraps eat spiders, slugs, crcikets and flies.

Venus flytraps can only be found on the coasts of North and South Carolina in the US.
